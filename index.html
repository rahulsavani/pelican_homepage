Research Interests: Game Theory, Algorithms & Complexity, Automated Trading, Agent-Based Modelling

I am a Reader in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Liverpool. I work on problems at the interface of economics/finance and computer science. I have worked extensively on equilibrium computation for game-theoretic models of strategic interaction. I have also worked as a consultant on a variety of algorithmic trading projects. Currently, I lead the Economics and Computation Research Group.
